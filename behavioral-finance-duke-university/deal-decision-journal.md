# Deal Decision Journal
## deal-decision-journal.md (PE/VC-Specific)

*Use this journal to audit real, mock, or historical deals. Write down your investment thesis BEFORE a deal closes, list the psychological biases you or your team might be falling for, and evaluate the decision-making process.*

---

### 📝 Deal Audit Template
```markdown
### 💼 Deal Name: [e.g., Project Atlas / Startup X Series A]
* **Date Audited:** YYYY-MM-DD
* **Deal Phase:** [Sourcing / Screening / DD / Valuation & Term Sheet / Post-Investment Operations]
* **Investment Thesis:**
  * [Why are we buying/backing this company?]
* **🔍 Psychological Biases Audited:**
  * **Narrative Fallacy:** [Are we buying a beautiful story or solid unit economics?]
  * **Similarity Bias:** [Does the founder remind us of ourselves/our partners?]
  * **Anchoring:** [Is our LBO model matching an arbitrary valuation set by the broker?]
  * **Herd Behavior / FOMO:** [Are we bidding higher because Tier 1 VCs are looking at it?]
  * **Overconfidence / Planning Fallacy:** [Are the projected revenue growth rates realistic (e.g., 80% YoY)?]
* **🛠️ Mitigation Actions Taken:**
  * [How did we neutralize these biases? e.g., third-party DD, walk-away pricing, red team analysis]
* **📊 Decision Outcome & Retrospective:**
  * [What happened? Did we pass? Did we win/lose the auction? What did we learn?]
```
